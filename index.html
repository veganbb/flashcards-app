Vegans Athletes

Patrik Baboumian - holds multiple world records in weight lifting, namely:

  • Log Lift: When weighing 105kg, Patrik broke the world record lifting 165kg
  • Fronthold: Held a 20kg weight with arms straight in-front for 86 seconds, beating the previous record by 10 seconds
  • Yoke Walk: Carried 555kg a distance of 10 meters in 28 seconds
  • Beer Keg: Lifted 150.2kg overhead

Kendrick Farris - was the only strongman sent to the 2016 Olympics for the USA, during which he broke the U.S record by lifting a total of 831 pounds (377 kg) -- 370 pounds (168 kg) in the snatch and 461 pounds (209 kg) in the clean and jerk

Scott Jurek - is an ultramarathon runner (ultramarathons start at distances of 26 miles), some notable accomplishments include:

  • winning Millwok 100k three times
  • holding the the record for the 2189 mile Appalachian Trail
  • setting the American 24 hour record with a run of 165.7 miles
  • setting ten ultramarathon course records
  • becoming the first American winner of the Spartathon (246k)

Carl Lewis - won 9 olympic medals and set world records in the 100 m, 4 x 100 m and 4 x 200 m relays
